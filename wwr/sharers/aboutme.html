About Me

I was born in Hong Kong, one of the most crowded cities in the world, and have been living in the high-density living environment over 30 years. It is always a headache for my family to keep our home tidy since we have a lot of stuffs but with limited spaces in our home. Thus, this was how the idea of "Sharers" came along.

I hope that we all together can accomplish something good to help out the people around us, and at the same time, not wasting anything resuable in our community!
