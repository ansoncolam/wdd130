About Me

I was born in Hong Kong, one of the most crowded cities in the world, and have been living in high-density living environment for over 30 years. It is always a headache for my family to keep our home tidy because we have a lot of stuffs but with limited spaces in our home. Thus, this is how "Sharers" idea came along. I hope that we together can accomplish something good to help out the people around us, and at the same time, not wasting anything resuable!
