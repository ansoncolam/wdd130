Overview

Purpose

3 main purposes: Introduce local attractions, share day-trip plans, and explain local customs in Hong Kong.

Hong Kong is not a large city comparing to New York or Los Angeles in the United States. However, Hong Kong is among the most densed cities in the world. Visitors may find it difficult to decide where they can go, what they can do, and how they can easily travel around in the city. My site provides suggestions or even recommendation with details for visitors to solve their problems. Therefore, it will no longer a headache for visitors to make trvel plan in Hong Kong anymore!

Audience

My target audience are those who are intested in or planning to visit Hong Kong, induding those visitors coming to Hong Kong for the first time and returned visitors.

Victoria Peak, locally known as The Peak, is a hill located in Central and Western District in Hong Kong. Since It is the highest hill on Hong Kong Island, The Peak is the must-go sightseeing spot where visitors can see the full view of Hong Kong. You can get there by bus or tram.

Colored paragraph example

Tips: it is better to get an Octopus card rental when you arrive Hong Kong the first day. Octopus card is a form of value-stored smart card for making various electronic payments in Hong Kong. It is accepted in most of the restaurants, convenience stores, and public transportations in Hong Kong.
